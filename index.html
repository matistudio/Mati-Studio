Who We Are

A Creative Powerhouse Where AI Meets Premium Design

I'm Mati the founder of Mati Studio. My journey began in 2022 as a freelance AI video creator on Fiverr, where I successfully delivered projects for over 200 clients. Today, I work directly with global brands, specializing in advanced AI video generation, precision prompting, and professional video editing.

Mati Studio blends cinematic AI content generation with high-end commercial aesthetics, so your brand gets the visuals it deserves without the cost of physical sets, hired crews, or lengthy production timelines. What once took weeks of planning, location scouting, and thousands of dollars in equipment can now be achieved with greater creative freedom and zero logistical headaches. The result is big-budget quality, delivered faster, at a fraction of the price. But beyond the cost savings, it means your brand is no longer limited by budget, location, or resources.
